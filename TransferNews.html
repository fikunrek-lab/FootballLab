Arsenal Transfer News

After winning the premier league trophy and ending their 22-year long drought, Arsenal still have some work to do if they aim to finally win the champions league. Here are the positions we need to upgrade ahead of a challenging season.

🎯 Ideal Positional Signings

RB (Right Back)

Arsenal should target a backup right back capable of allowing Jurrien Timber more rest throughout the season. This player should be comfortable in attacking areas and possess enough quality to start Champions League matches when required.

LB (Left Back)

A backup left back is needed to provide competition for Piero Hincapié. This assumes Riccardo Calafiori's long-term future under Mikel Arteta is at left centre-back rather than left back.

DM/CM (Defensive Midfielder / Central Midfielder)

Arsenal need another midfielder capable of rotating with Declan Rice. The ideal profile should be athletic, capable of covering large spaces defensively, and maintaining intensity when Rice is unavailable.

DM (Deep-Lying Playmaker)

A deep-lying playmaker would provide Arsenal with additional solutions during build-up. This profile should improve progression through the central corridor and help unlock compact defensive blocks.

LW (Left Winger)

Arsenal need a starting-quality left winger capable of providing a similar level of threat to Bukayo Saka. This would improve tactical flexibility, reduce Arsenal's reliance on the right flank, and make attacks more unpredictable for opposing defences.
